Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things we hear.

What's included in your service bundles?
+

Each plan includes website management, social media, email marketing, SEO optimization, analytics, and strategic guidance. The difference is in the depth of service and number of platforms we manage. All plans come with dedicated support and monthly performance reports.

Do you work with nonprofits and startups?
+

Absolutely! We specialize in working with mission-driven organizations. We offer nonprofit pricing and are experienced with grant requirements, donor communication, and mission-driven messaging. We've helped over 150 nonprofits grow their digital presence.

How long before we see results?
+

You'll see improvements to website analytics and social engagement within 30-60 days. SEO results typically take 3-6 months. Lead and revenue impact varies based on your business model, but we typically see significant improvements by month 6. We track everything and report monthly.

Can I change plans or scale up later?
+

Yes! We encourage clients to start with what fits their budget and scale as they see results. Many of our Starter clients move to Growth after 3-6 months. You can adjust your plan with 30 days notice.

What if I'm not happy with the results?
+

We provide a 60-day satisfaction guarantee on all services. If you're not seeing the impact we've outlined, we'll either adjust our strategy or refund your service fee. Your success is our priority, and we're confident in our approach.

How is data security and confidentiality handled?
+

We take security seriously. All client data is encrypted, backed up daily, and never shared with third parties. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and industry best practices. You retain full ownership of all assets and data we create.

Do you offer custom solutions outside these plans?
+

Definitely. For larger organizations, specialized needs, or white-label solutions, we create custom packages. Contact us for a consultation and we'll design something that fits your unique situation perfectly.

How do you measure success?
+

We align metrics with your business goals. For e-commerce, that's revenue. For nonprofits, it's donors and engagement. For service businesses, it's qualified leads. We set KPIs upfront, track them weekly, and report monthly. Transparency is built into everything we do.
